AiSha LLC extends this philosophy to chassis reinforcement, brakes and suspension components. We use trusted suppliers like Techno Toy Tuning who have been proving the quality of their work on the track for decades. Our fully integrated, retro-futurist center console, and fully modern ultra-reliable electronics and high fidelity sound system paired with an onboard, top of the line NVIDIA GPU creates a worry-free driving experience like you never imagined possible in a classic sports car, all while enhancing the character of a well-built Nissan L engine.
